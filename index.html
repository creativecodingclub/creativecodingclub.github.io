About
The Club

Hello friend! Welcome! We’re Cassidy and Cami Williams, leaders of the Creative Coding Club.

The idea for the club began in December 2016 after the club’s founder, Nat Cooper, attended an awesome Web Animation Workshop with Sarah Drasner and Val Head. She left the workshop feeling so inspired that she made a promise to herself to build a new creative project every month in 2017. After making her own accountability-buddy system, she realized that more could benefit from having a creative coding community, too. Thus, the Creative Coding Club was born!

The club has over 800 members making awesome things every month. Join in, we’d love to have you!

How It Works

The first Friday of every month, a theme will be announced via email. That email will share curated inspiration, resources, and tools to get your creative juices flowing!

Club members gather online in our Slack channel to share inspiration, resources and progress on our projects for that theme. Submissions for the theme are due before the next theme is announced.

Projects are gathered on Slack and will be publicly shared and promoted with the larger tech community! All formats are accepted- CodePen, GitHub, YouTube, you name it, we take it!

Themes

Always open for interpretation and are loosely based on an event that happens that month.

Club members are encouraged to create their own unique project, and to challenge themselves to build on a new skill or technology they have not yet explored.
